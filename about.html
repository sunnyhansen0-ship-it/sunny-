About Me

I'm Sunny Hansen, a student currently enrolled at Swinburne University pursuing a degree with a major in Cyber Security. I'm passionate about understanding how digital systems work — and more importantly, how to protect them.

My background

I chose Cyber Security because I've always been curious about how things work under the hood. From figuring out why a network drops out to reading about real-world data breaches, I find the field genuinely fascinating. Swinburne has given me a strong foundation in both the technical and theoretical sides of security.

Alongside my degree, I've been building practical skills through online labs, capture-the-flag challenges, and personal projects. Every problem I solve teaches me something new about how systems can be both attacked and defended.

What drives me

Cyber Security

My major and my passion. I want to understand threats from every angle — from phishing attacks to network intrusions — so I can build better defences.

Ethical hacking

Learning to think like an attacker to defend like a pro. I enjoy working through CTF challenges and exploring how vulnerabilities are discovered and patched.

Continuous learning

The security landscape changes every day. I stay current by following industry news, completing online courses, and practising in safe lab environments.

Outside of study

When I'm not studying or working on labs, I enjoy gaming, keeping up with the latest tech news, and spending time outdoors. I'm also part of a study group at Swinburne where we work through security problems together.
